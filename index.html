Server Guidelines

Minecraft Rules

01
No Hacking & Cheating
Unfair mods, hacked clients, X-ray packs, and auto-clickers are strictly banned from Aethel SMP. Play fair so everyone can enjoy a balanced experience.
Allowed: Optifine, Sodium, Minimaps (no player radar)
02
No Griefing & Stealing
Do not destroy or take items from another player's builds, even if the land is unclaimed. Respect other players' hard work and creative creations.
03
No Chat Spam
Avoid flooding the chat with capital letters, highly repetitive messages, or pointless symbols. Keep the public server chat pleasant, clean, and legible.
04
No Lag Machines
Do not build redstone clocks or complex automated mob farms intentionally designed to lag, freeze, or crash the server database and performance.
