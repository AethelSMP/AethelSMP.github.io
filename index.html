The Code

Server Rules

A short, fair set of rules keeps Aethel welcoming for everyone.

No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind. Treat everyone the way you'd want to be treated. Toxicity ruins worlds — we don't tolerate it.

Hacked clients, x-ray, duping, and macro auto-clickers are strictly banned. Report exploits to staff instead of abusing them.

Do not destroy or steal from other players' builds without consent. PvP is allowed in marked zones only. Protect your base and respect others'.

No spam, advertising, or excessive caps. Keep public chat friendly and English where possible so staff can moderate fairly.

Alt accounts used to bypass bans or gain unfair advantages are not allowed. Play fair — your reputation is your legacy on Aethel.
